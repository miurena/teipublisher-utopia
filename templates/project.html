ENCODING AND TOOLS

The edition uses the TEI/XML language for the encoding of the texts. The texts are aligned using the <s> and <seg> elements, @xml:id, and @corresp. Each chapter and the four texts are contained together in one single document. Thus, there are nine documents in total plus another one containing the paratexts of Medinilla’s edition.

Visualization tool:

The visualization tool used is TEIPublisher. The functions integrated in the present edition have already been used in other projects like the Van Gogh Letters and others (functions to be identified). It is integrated within the webpage.
